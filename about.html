ABOUT


The UNC Women's Club Lacrosse team competes in the Division I Chesapeake conference of the Mid-Atlantic Women's Lacrosse League (MAWLL) governed by the Women's Club Lacrosse Assocation (WCLA). We are proud to compete in the most competitive league in the nation, facing rivals such as Delaware, Georgia and UVA before moving onto the Regional and National tournaments.

This team is self-led and motivated to win. We represent UNC by striving to reflect their core values.

When you're a Women's Lacrosse Tar Heel, you're much more than a teammate... you are family.

Practice

We practice at Finely Field #2 three times a week:
Monday, Wednesday and Thursday from 5:30PM-7:30PM.

Practice consists of fitness, strength, drills and scrimmage play.

Games/Tournaments

Fall: This is our off season. We typically have one playday or tournament and 2-3 local games.

Spring: This is our time to compete. We play in 3-4 tournaments at schools across the East coast including the CHILL tournament hosted here at UNC. If we qualify, we then go on to play in the Regional and National tournament.

Nationals

Each year, the WCLA hosts the National tournament at a new city in the US. Our team has qualified for, and competed in, the Division I national tournmaent in both 2017 and 2018, placing 4th in the nation.

Our last national championship was in 2015, when we won the Division II national title.

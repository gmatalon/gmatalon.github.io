INTERESTED IN JOINING?


The University of North Carolina Women's Club Lacrosse team is one of the best programs in the country, finishing in the top 4 for the last two years. Our team requires a serious time commitment but the sense of community and support makes it worthwhile!

Practice is 3 nights a week with tournaments during various weekends in the spring.

If you are interested, we want to hear from you! Please contact us and come to tryouts ready to play!

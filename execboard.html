EXEC BOARD

The Women's Club Lacrosse Exec Board supports the team, the players and their families.

We strive to create an organized, fun and inclusive environment for our players. Our goal is to keep the team running, whether that means planning practices, booking hotel rooms or raising money for nationals. We want the players to only have to focus on lacrosse and enjoy what they love to do! The exec board upholds the integrity of our team values while also keeping in check with UNC club regulations.
